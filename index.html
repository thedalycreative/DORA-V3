About DORA

Complete In-Home Healthcare

DORA offers personalised, in-home health and wellbeing support for individuals living with permanent disabilities — with specialised care for spinal injuries. Our services are built on compassion, reliability, and lived experience.

Founded with the vision of dignified care that meets people where they are, we prioritise comfort, independence, and respect in every service we provide.

Whether it’s daily support, mobility assistance, or medical care planning, DORA is here to help you live well — at home, on your terms.
