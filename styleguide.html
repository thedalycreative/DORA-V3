BRAND STYLE GUIDE [version 4])

Dora Health & Wellbeing

Brand Voice & Tone

Voice: Calm, clear, confident, and compassionate. We speak like a trusted guide—never overwhelming, always grounded. Whether explaining complex health systems or offering encouragement, we do so with warmth, clarity, and care.

Personality: Human-first, thoughtful, intuitive. We avoid jargon and prioritise emotional intelligence. Our language is empowering, not patronising—because our clients are people, not conditions.

Visual Identity: All visuals must reflect our commitment to dignified care. We favour polished, professional images of real environments, captured in a soft, approachable, and respectful way—never clinical or staged.

Brand Values

Empowering Independence

We help participants direct their own lives through personalised, dignified support that fosters skills and autonomy.

Consistent Excellence & Trust

We build confidence through reliable, clear communication and exceptional care—especially in spinal injury expertise.

Dignity & People-First Care

We treat each individual with respect and compassion, recognising their story and humanity in every interaction.

Holistic Wellbeing

We design care that supports physical, emotional, and social health—collaborating with participants and families.

Mission

"Empowering lives through dignified care and visual clarity."

We deliver compassionate in-home services that honour independence and individuality, with clinical and lived experience at the core—specialising in spinal injuries and complex needs.

Vision

We aim to redefine in-home care by empowering individuals with personalised support that fosters fulfilment and autonomy. Through relationships, expertise, and holistic wellbeing, we raise the standard of home healthcare.

"Dignity Always"

Every design choice honours the individual. Calm, clear, and centred on real lives.
