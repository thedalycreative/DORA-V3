Vision

DORA exists to transform the way people experience aged care, disability services, and personal support – by making it more human, more thoughtful, and more respectful. We believe in care that connects.

Mission

Our mission is to deliver personalised support services that honour each individual’s story. Through intuitive platforms, consistent care, and a calm, confident tone, we support lives with dignity, clarity and warmth.

Business Values

  • Human-first: Care is not just a task – it’s a relationship.
  • Empowerment through clarity: We simplify complexity with calm, clear guidance.
  • Consistency & trust: Support you can rely on, from real people who listen.
  • Dignity always: Everyone deserves to feel seen, heard, and respected.

Tone of Voice

We speak with warmth, clarity, and calm authority. Think of the tone of a supportive nurse who knows the way forward but never rushes the person in her care. Empathy leads, simplicity follows.
